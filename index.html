 Guitars for the Senses
Visually, it should invite touch.
Looking at it, you should want to pick it up.
Its touch should invite playing.
Making music with it should come as naturally as picking it up.
Its sound should compel you to play - to listen.
I love to handcraft every part of that appeal.
